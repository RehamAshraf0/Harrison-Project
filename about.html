About Kitchen Together

Simply put, this is who we are: an Australian company that offers turn-key delivery of modern kitchens and wardrobes to families in Brisbane and the Gold Coast.

Does this paint a full picture though? Read on to discover the secrets of our identity and how they translate into the carefully crafted service that we offer to anyone in need of a new kitchen.

Our Values

Along the way you will receive a unique set of benefits.

Honesty

it's not just the best policy. It's a core business practice to act in a transparent, trustworthy manner that earns the respect of colleagues, customers and the public.

Accountability

Accepting responsibility for your actions (and inactions) is the ultimate way to build trust internally and externally.

Promise to customers

Creating a great customer experience begins with staying true to the words we speak and the bonds we make.

Passion

Having a joy not just for the work itself but also the people around us, so that everyone can be bold, innovative and creative.

Quality

Companies are judged by the craftmanship of their products and services, so the highest standards must be maintained.
